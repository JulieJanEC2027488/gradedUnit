The idea of the animation is taken from medieval Bohemia legend about the Charles’ bridge that it’s situated in the center of Prague. I was inspirited by my latest holiday in the Czech Republic, where I have been in Prague Centre a lot. One of the most inspiring trips was to the exhibition about King Charles IV., and it made me more aware of how he helped the people progress and build the city. The animation supposes to introduce one of the old legends from the 14th Century about building the bridge. This will demonstrate my love for history and medieval illustration with pride in my hometown.
