TROPICAL FISH GAME

The game is inspired by the original Tropical Fish game
launched in 1985 from a series of Nintendo Game & Watch.
Player aims to catch jumping fish from the aquarium
(on the right side) and transfer it to the opposite aquarium
with a fishbowl
Player can move only left to right by clicking
on buttons (player is moved a little bit to
the chosen direction)
When the fish is caught – the player quickly
transfers the fish to the aquarium on the left side
Player can’t catch another fish while
he is holding one in the fishbowl
When fish die (fall on the ground) – player loses one heart
If the player let the fish die while no heart
remains – the game is over
The main aim of the game is to get a higher
score before 4 fish die

INSTRUCTION

LEFT button - player moves LEFT
RIGHT button - player moves RIGHT
GOAL:
Catch fish and transfer it to
opposite aquarium!
YOU CAN TRANSFER ONE FISH AT THE TIME!
If fish fall on the group
You loose one life ! :(
